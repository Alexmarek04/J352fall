New York City is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. It features many attractions and landmarks for visitors of all ages to see. Tourists can take a short ferry ride for optimal viewing of the Statue of Liberty, and learn about its historical significance. Other notable attractions include the Empire State Building and the Brooklyn Bridge. For those who like to spend time outside, Central Park and Washington Square Park are great for people-watching and strolling. Times Square is one of the worls most visited attracitions, with over 50 million visitors annually.
